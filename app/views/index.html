Tell DVLA about your medical condition

Use this service to:

  • tell DVLA about your diabetes

If you need to tell DVLA about a different condition, download and complete the paper form – then post it to DVLA.

Don’t worry

90% of drivers are allowed to retain their current licence.

What you'll need to complete this service
  • your car or motorcycle driver’s licence
  • your healthcare professional (e.g. GP, Nurse, Consultant) details
  • your medication details

Tell DVLA about your medical condition

Use this service to:

  • renew your medical driving licence

Don’t worry

90% of drivers are allowed to retain their current licence or are issued a medical driving licence subject to review on renewal.

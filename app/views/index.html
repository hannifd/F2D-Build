Tell DVLA about your medical condition

You can use this service to tell DVLA about your medical condition.

It is a legal requirement to tell DVLA of a medical condition that might affect your driving.

Don’t worry

Most drivers are allowed to continue driving.

What you'll need

  • to be a resident in Great Britain - there is a different process in Northern Ireland, Isle of Man and the Channel Islands
  • your healthcare professionals' (eg GP, nurse, consultant) details

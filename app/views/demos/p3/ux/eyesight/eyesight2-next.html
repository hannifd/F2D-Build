The legal eyesight standard

  • you must be able to read a car number plate from 20 metres
  • you must not have been told by a doctor or optician that your eyesight is currently worse than 6/12 (decimal 0.5) on the Snellen scale

If you are in any doubt, you should discuss with your optician or doctor.

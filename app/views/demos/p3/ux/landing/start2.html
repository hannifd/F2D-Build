Tell the DVLA about your health condition

Use this service to:

  • tell the DVLA about a health condition for the first time
  • tell the DVLA if your condition has changed
  • renew your short-term licence

This service is currently only suitable if you’re a telling the DVLA about Diabetes or Glaucoma (Why can’t I report other conditions?). If you want to tell the DVLA of different condition, please use the postal service.

Start now

What you’ll need

  • your Car or Motorcycle Photocard Driver’s Licence
  • your Health Provider Details
  • your medication details

Don't Worry!

Over 90% of drivers who tell DVLA of a relevant condition either keep their current licence or are issued with a short period licence so that their condition can be regularly reviewed.

Other ways to apply

By post

Please provide the following:
  • your full name and address
  • your driving licence number (or your date of birth if you do not know your driver number)
  • details about your medical condition

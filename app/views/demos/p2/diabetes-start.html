How many severe episodes of hypoglycaemia have you had in the last 12 months?

What is a severe episode of hypoglycaemia?
A severe hypoglycaemic episode is one which requires assistance from another person or persons.

Do you get the warning symptoms of hypoglycaemia?

What are the warning symptoms?

Hypoglycaemia causes symptoms such as

  • hunger
  • shakiness
  • nervousness
  • sweating
  • dizziness or light-headedness
  • sleepiness
  • confusion
  • difficulty speaking
  • anxiety
  • weakness

Hypoglycaemia can also happen during sleep. Some signs of hypoglycaemia during sleep include:

  • crying out or having nightmares
  • finding pyjamas or sheets damp from perspiration
  • feeling tired, irritable, or confused after waking up

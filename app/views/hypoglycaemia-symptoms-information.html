Symptoms of Hypoglycaemia

As you are a driver with insulin-treated diabetes, you need to know what the symptoms of hypoglycaemia are. Examples are shown below.

  • hunger
  • shakiness
  • nervousness
  • sweating
  • dizziness or light-headedness
  • sleepiness
  • confusion
  • difficulty speaking
  • anxiety
  • weakness

Hypoglycaemia can also happen during sleep. Some signs include

  • crying out or having nightmares
  • finding pyjamas or sheets damp from perspiration
  • feeling tired, irritable, or confused after waking up
